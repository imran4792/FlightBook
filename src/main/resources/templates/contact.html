Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?

Simply search for flights using our search form, select your preferred flight, enter passenger details, and complete the payment process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking through your account dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by airline.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets for secure payments.

How do I get my boarding pass?

Download your boarding pass from your account dashboard 24 hours before departure, or collect it at the airport.
